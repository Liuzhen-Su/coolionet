About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I'm now studying for a doctorate in computer science under the guidance of Professor Lihua Zhang in Fudan University. And I received my bachelor degree from Shandong University in 2019.

I have great interest and enthusiasm in the field of artificial intelligence.
